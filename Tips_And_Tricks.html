Tips and Tricks for those better bakes.

Below we have listed a few tips and tricks that we have tried and tested to ensure the best outomes!

Sauteeing your onions taking too long?
Add a little water, and cover with a lid! The moisture from the water as it evaporates will help to soften those onions in no time! This also works with garlic and other veggies!

Want perfectly circular cookies every time?
Once you remove the tray from the oven, immediately grab a cup or circular cookie cutter larger than your cookie, and gently rotate it around the cookie whilst still warm! This will help to create the perfectly circular shape before they cool down and harden.

Want perfect scrambled eggs every time?
Add a little butter to your pan as you scramble, and remove from the heat just before they finish cooking. They make look under cooked at first, but eggs carry on cooking in the residual heat from the pan! Afer 30-60 seconds off the heat, they should be perfectly fluffy and light!

Struggling with your bread dough being too sticky? Flour not helping?
Wet your hands before you fold the dough, this will prevent the dough sticking to your skin. You can use water for most regular breads, or oil for doughs like a focaccia!

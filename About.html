About us

Who we are, and why we created this blog.

Hey! Our names are Amber and Benjamin, and we created this blog to share our cooking creations with others, who may be lacking inspiration or creativity. Our goal was to try and show people who may lack confidence in the kitchen that it is not an intimidating area to work in!

We both have experience working in kitchens, where Amber studied catering for four years and worked prepping for a school cafeteria, and Ben worked in the kitchen of a private gym making meals for patrons before or after their workouts! We both really enjoy cooking and baking from scratch with fresh ingredients, and love to view cooking as a fun activity where creativity can flourish.

By creating this blog, we hoped to share our quick meals, tips and tricks, and handy advice for creating delicious and nutritious meals in the comfort of your own home, without having to set aside hours for prep or spending hundreds on equipement. Please, enjoy your stay and we hope you learn so much!
